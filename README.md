## ðŸš¨ What CareLink Solves  
Every year, uncontrolled epilepsy causes:

- Delayed first aid in **70%+** of adolescent seizures  
- Increased injury, hospitalization & SUDEP risk  
- Poor communication between peers, families & clinicians  
- Lack of structured escalation workflows in hostels/dorms  

CareLink closes this gap through **real-time seizure alerts**, **family escalation**, **peer-to-peer fallback**, and **EHR-integrated follow-ups**.
